Dynamics Of Machine
Experiment No. 08
To study the free vibration of two rotor system and to determine the natural frequency of vibration theoretically and experimentally.

Apparatus
Shaft, Stopwatch.


Description of Set up
The general arrangement for carrying out the experiment is as shown in Fig.1, Two discs having different mass moment of inertia are clamped one at each end of shaft by means of collect and chucks. Attaching the cross lever weights can change mass moment of inertia of any disc. Both discs are free to oscillate in the ball bearings. This provides negligible damping during experiment.

Procedure
  1. Fix the two discs to the shaft and fit the shaft in bearings.
  2. Deflect the discs in opposite direction by hand and release.
  3. Note down time required for particular number of oscillations.
  4. Fit the cross arm to one of the disc say B and again note down time.
  5. Repeat the procedure with different equal masses attached to the end of cross arm and note down the time.

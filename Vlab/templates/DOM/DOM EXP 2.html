Dynamics Of Machine
Experiment No. 02
To determine the Radius of Gyration ‘K’ of given compound pendulum and To verify the relation

Apparatus
Compound Pendulum, Scale, Stopwatch, Thread.


Description of Set up
The compound pendulum consists of steel rod. The bar is supported in the hole by the knife – edge. It is possible to change the length of suspended pendulum by supporting the bar in different holes.

Procedure
  1. Support the rod in any one of the holes
  2. Note the length of suspended pendulum and determine OG
  3. Allow the ball to oscillate and determine the periodic time ‘T’ by knowing the time for say 10 oscillations.
  4. Repeat the experiment with different length of suspension.
  5. Complete the observation table given below.
